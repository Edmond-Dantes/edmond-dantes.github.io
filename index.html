Edmond Akpan

Overview

"Currently losing sleep learning cool stuff"

Edmond Akpan is a junior web developer who started his career in July 2018 with a small housing tech startup called Tokyo Hearth, currently AtHearth, based in Tokyo, Japan.

He graduated from the Tokyo Le Wagon bootcamp mid June 2018 where he studied Ruby on Rails. As part of a team of four, he lead the development of www.hellokanji.com, a Japanese language study app.

Before becoming a professional web developer, Edmond taught English for 5 years in the Kanagawa prefecture of Japan while studying coding fundamentals. While teaching himself coding, he spent three of the 5 years focusing on building a few iOS apps in React Native and Swift. Most notably, Space Reflex : Fast Twitch Alien Galaxy Pinball.
